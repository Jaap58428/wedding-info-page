‚úÖ What's there (and what isn't)

This is not your ordinary camping (or even hotel), you're signing up for an adventure ;). So please be aware there are some differences to what you might expect. For your convenience we listed some of the most important points here. If you have any specific questions, please contact us.

The following items will be freely available to you:

  • One- or two-person bed (including bedlinen)
  • Parking
  • Shared fridges and shared outdoor kitchen
  • A few things for breakfast on Saturday. Variety will be very limited, so feel free to bring some yums yourself (can be stored in shared fridges)
  • All food and drinks from the moment we come back from the church ceremony on Saturday
  • Hot Tub (space for 2 people and needs to be heated up with wood by the people who want to use it.)
  • Transport to the church (and back of course)
  • Coins (4x) for taking a shower
  • Fun

The following items will NOT BE INCLUDED!! Keep this in mind when preparing for your visit.

  • Dinner on Friday - here is a list of restaurants in the area
  • Lunch on Saturday
  • Any food or drinks on Sunday (except for potential leftovers)
  • Towels
  • Wi-Fi
  • Some accommodations don't have electricity (they do have light)
  • Mirrors (except at the showers)
  • Showers and regular toilets directly on the venue grounds (see below)
  • Dinosaurs
